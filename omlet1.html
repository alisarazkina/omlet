OMLETIK-LICO

Fluffy omelet like in childhood Those same magnificent omelettes cooked according to all the precepts of proper nutrition. This recipe is still relevant today. Ingredients (three servings) ? Chicken egg - five pieces; ? Milk - one glass; ? Butter - two teaspoons; ? Salt - to taste. Cooking In a bowl, beat the eggs with a whisk until smooth. Add milk to eggs and mix thoroughly. Salt to taste. Pour our mixture into a mold pre-greased with butter and send it to the oven. We cook an omelette at 190 degrees for about half an hour. Ready omelet can be greased with butter.
