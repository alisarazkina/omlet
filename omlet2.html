OMLETIK

Prepare the ingredients for the recipe. Step 2 of 9 eggs in a bowl Break the eggs into a suitable bowl and stir with a whisk. You do not need to beat for a long time, the main thing is to achieve uniformity. Step 3 of 9 agitation If you choose homemade products when choosing ingredients, the egg mixture will be a bright orange color.Salt a little and pour in the milk. Choose the percentage of fat according to your taste, in this recipe this moment is not important. But keep in mind, the fatter the foods, the higher the calorie content of the omelette. Step 5 of 9 adding cheese chips Grate hard cheese, set aside a little for serving, and add the rest to a bowl. As for cheese, take your favorite. After all, it is his taste that will be felt in the finished dish. Step 6 of 9 pouring eggs into a frying pan Melt the butter in a frying pan and pour in the omelet "dough".Cover with a lid, make medium heat and cook the dish for 5-7 minutes. Step 8 of 9 omelet with cheese in a pan During this time, the egg mass should rise and seize well, not a single wet area will remain on its surface and you will not have to turn it over to the other side. Step 9 of 9 portion of the dish A tender and very tasty omelette with milk and cheese in a pan is ready.
